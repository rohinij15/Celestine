JERRY'S JEWLERY STORE

Who are we

Jerry McGuire started his first Jewelry store in 1942 and ever since then has been opening up shops all over the United States. We sell antique jewlery that were refurbished into a much higher quality.

What we offer

We offer anything, from high end 24 karat gold neckleces to good quality children's rings with fun little designs
