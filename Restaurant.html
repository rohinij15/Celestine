SUE'S CAFE

Who are we

My grandma Sue Sylvester was a strong women. She loved loved loved her coffee and donuts. Her dream has always been to start a cafe but she never go the chance. Her passing left a hole in my heart, I wanted to fufil her dream so I dedicated this cafe to her. Welcome to Sue's Cafe.

What we offer

In our cafe we sell a multitude of things, varying from bagels to tea. Everything we sell is made right here at the shop. We have some very talented bakers. I could garuntee you that once you come to Sue's cafe you won't be able to stop.

Covid-19 precautions

In our store we take Covid precations very seriously.

*All employees will wear masks
*Our staff will practice social distancing
*We wipe our counters and chairs with disinfected wipes after each customer leaves
*We have hand-sanitizer stations around the store
*We only allow customers to get take out or eat outside
*We accept online orders and will have them delivered to you in a safely and timely manner

We ask customers to:

*Practice social distancing
*Wear masks
*Be respectful
